About me

My name is Carolina Flores and I am in my second year reading for a degree in Mathematics and Philosophy at the University of Oxford.

I would like to pursue further study in philosophy, with a focus on philosophy of mind in the context of current neuroscience. Some of my other current areas of interest are Philosophy of Mathematics and Early Modern Philosophy, but as so many other philosophers, my interests range widely - the nature improves philosophy is such that one's understanding of any area gains by studying others.

I also hope to continue to do work and advocacy in the area of Effective Altruism.

I grew up in Portugal and moved to the UK to study.

In my free time, I enjoy running, going to hipster gigs, baking, and drinking with friends (more often than not, tea).
