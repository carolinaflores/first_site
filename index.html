About me

I am a second year student reading for a degree in Maths and Philosophy at the University of Oxford.

I grew up in Portugal and moved to the UK to study.

I would like to pursue a career in philosophy, with a focus on philosophy of mind in the context of scientific discoveries. I also hope to continue to do work and advocacy in the area of Effective Altruism.

In my free time, I enjoy running, going to hipster gigs, baking, and drinking with friends (more often than not, tea).
